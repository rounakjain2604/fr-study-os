This app is a practical CA Final operating system, not a motivation dashboard. It is syllabus-first, progress-aware, daily-execution-focused, and intentionally separates lecture coverage from exam readiness.

- Daily Cockpit as the first screen

- Detailed actionable FR/AFM sprint from 25 May 2026
- Local persistence via browser localStorage

## Study Philosophy

First pass means structure, lectures/notes, core concepts, selected examples, and chapter recognition.

Exam mastery means timed ICAI-style/faculty Q Bank solving, presentation formats, formula recall, mixed-topic handling, traps, fast revision, and tested readiness.

The app must keep those two states separate.
