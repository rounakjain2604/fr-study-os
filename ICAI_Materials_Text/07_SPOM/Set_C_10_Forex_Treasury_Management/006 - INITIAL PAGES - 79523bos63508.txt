         BEFORE WE BEGIN …


The Board of Studies, ICAI created the study materials for Self-Paced Online Module-Set C with
the goal of aligning the curriculum with the International Education Standards (IESs) of the
International Federation of Accountants (IFAC) to instill and strengthen the prerequisite skills
required to become a well-rounded, capable, and globally competitive accounting professional.
Additionally, this study guide emphasizes the National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020)
initiatives, such as curriculum restructuring and new pedagogical approaches centered on the
use of technology in the classroom, as well as conceptual clarity as an alternative to rote
learning.
Our economic policies have been liberalized and globalized to be in line with the changes
occurring throughout  the  world. This has  led  to the convergence  of several  significant
developments in the field of Forex and Treasury Management that are required to boost the
economy as well as the corporate sector.
Requirement of Professional Knowledge and Skills
It is required of you to not only gain professional knowledge after Intermediate Level but also
the capacity to put that information to use in addressing challenges and solving real-world
scenarios prior to appearing in Final Level. You can acquire the professional values, ethics, and
attitudes, as well as the needed technical competence and professional abilities, by pursuing
an integrated learning approach that combines academic education with hands-on training.
Significant Components of Forex and Treasury Management
Elective Paper of Set C – Forex and Treasury Management is a blend of theory and practical.
The Study Material consists of Chapter 1 to 5 as mentioned in the syllabus.
The study material is prepared by keeping in mind the current scenarios in the Forex and
Treasury Management, wherever required. Similarly, students have also been familiarized with
the various concepts of Treasury Management. In the chapter, Organizational Structure of
Treasury, the  front, back and  mid-office have been discussed along  with the  role and
responsibilities of treasurer.
Moreover,  in  the  Chapter,  Process  of  Treasury,  system,  and  techniques  of  treasury
management,  dealing and  trading  operations,  liquidity management and  dealing room
operations have been lucidly explained.

Furthermore, in the chapter Types of Treasuries, basic background of the treasury and treasury
banking have also been included to give readers a working knowledge of these concepts.
Also, in the penultimate chapter, Treasury - Domestic, various topics have been discussed such
as negotiated dealing system, money market instruments, government securities, fixed income
securities,  liquidity adjustment  facility (LAF), linkage  of domestic operations with foreign
operations etc. And, finally, the last chapter, Treasury  - Forex has been added to give the
readers a conceptual clarity of the important concepts of forex, international treasury, derivative
markets, and interest rate derivatives.
