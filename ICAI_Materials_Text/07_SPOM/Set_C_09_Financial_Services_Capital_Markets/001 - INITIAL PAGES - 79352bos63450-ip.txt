         BEFORE WE BEGIN …


The Board of Studies, ICAI created the study materials for Self-Paced Online Module-Set C with
the goal of aligning the curriculum with the International Education Standards (IESs) of the
International Federation of Accountants (IFAC) to instill and strengthen the prerequisite skills
required to become a well-rounded, capable, and globally competitive accounting professional.
Additionally, this study guide emphasizes the National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020)
initiatives, such as curriculum restructuring and new pedagogical approaches centered on the
use of technology in the classroom, as well as conceptual clarity as an alternative to rote
learning.
Our economic policies have been liberalized and globalized to be in line with the changes
occurring throughout  the  world. This has  led  to the convergence  of several  significant
developments in the  field  of Financial Services and Capital Markets that are required  to
boost the economy as well as the corporate sector.

Requirement of Professional Knowledge and Skills

It is required of you to not only gain professional knowledge after Intermediate Level but also
the capacity to put that information to use in addressing challenges and solving real-world
scenarios prior to appearing in Final Level. You can acquire the professional values, ethics, and
attitudes, as well as the needed technical competence and professional abilities, by pursuing
an integrated learning approach that combines academic education with hands-on training.

Significant Components of Financial Services and Capital Markets

Elective Paper 9 of Set C – Financial Services and Capital Markets is a blend of theory and
practical. The Study Material consists of Chapter 1 to 16 as mentioned in the syllabus.
The study material is prepared by keeping in mind the current scenarios in the financial services
and capital markets operating, wherever, required. Similarly, students will also get familiarized
with the various concepts of capital market. In the chapter, Capital Market – Primary, besides
the main topics covered in this field such as Green Shoe Option, Application Supported by
Blocked Amount (ASBA), Anchor Investors etc., a relatively new concept in India – Special
Purpose Acquisition Companies (ASBA) has also been introduced.
Moreover, in the Chapter, Capital Market - Secondary, certain interesting topics such as Straight
