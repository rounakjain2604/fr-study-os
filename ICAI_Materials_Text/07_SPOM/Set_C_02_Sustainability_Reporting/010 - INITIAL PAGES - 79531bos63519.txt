         BEFORE WE BEGIN …


The contents of the Study Material for Self–paced Online Module of Set C Paper 2: Sustainable
Development and Sustainability Reporting have been designed and developed by the Board of
Studies,  ICAI,  with a  view  to  lay  the  foundation  for  future  specialisation  in  the area  of
Sustainability. The Study Material has been developed considering the National Education Policy
2020 (NEP 2020) recommendations emphasizing the conceptual clarity rather than rote learning.
Emphasis is also placed on fostering the application and analytical skills by solving Multiple Choice
Questions provided at the end of each chapter.  In line with the NEP 2020 recommendation on
technology-based learning, the Study Material would be supplemented by e-lectures facilitating the
experiential learning.
In recent years, the concept of sustainability has transcended mere buzzwords to become a
cornerstone  of  global  discourse  across  various  sectors.   The  importance  of  sustainable
development cannot be overstated, as  it is crucial for ensuring the long-term well-being of both
current and future generations. From environmental conservation to economic development and
social equity, sustainability has emerged as a guiding principle for shaping a more harmonious and
resilient future for humanity and the planet we inhabit.
The syllabus of this paper on ‘Sustainable Development and Sustainability Reporting’ comprises of
Fundamentals  of  Sustainable  Development,  Sustainability  Reporting   in  India,  Business
Responsibility and  Sustainability Reporting (BRSR)  in  India, Global Trends  in  Sustainability
Reporting and Sustainability Assurance
Requirement of Professional Knowledge and Skills

Students are required to learn and qualify Self-paced Online Modules after passing Intermediate
Examination but before appearing in Final Examination. Accordingly, they are expected to not only
acquire professional knowledge but also the ability to apply such knowledge in addressing issues
and problem solving. The integrated process of learning through academic education and practical
training will help inculcate in them, the requisite technical competence and professional skills.
Framework of Chapters: Uniform Structure comprising of specific components

Efforts have been made to present the content of ‘Sustainable Development and Sustainability
Reporting’ in a lucid manner. Care has been taken to present the chapters in a logical sequence
to facilitate easy understanding by the students.  Each chapter of the Study Material has been
structured uniformly and comprises of the following components:
