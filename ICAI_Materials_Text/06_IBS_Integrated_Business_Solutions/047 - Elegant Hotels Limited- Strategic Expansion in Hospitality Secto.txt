====================== PAGE 28 ======================


Economic Value Added
(EVA)
Step 1: NOPAT (Net Operating Profit After Tax)
  EBIT = ₹2 croresTax @30% = ₹0.60 crores
  NOPAT = ₹1.40 crores

Step 2: Capital Charge
   Invested Capital = ₹20 crores
  WACC = 8% → Capital Charge = ₹1.60 crores

EVA = NOPAT – Capital Charge = ₹1.40 – ₹1.60 = ₹(0.20)
   crores

   EVA is negative – indicating value erosion in FY 2026–27
